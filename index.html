Welcome to TinkerHub KMEA

We are a community of tinkerers, makers, policy geeks & students and are working towards mapping and empowering people who share a passion to innovate.

About Us

TinkerHub KMEA is one of the most active and collaborative campus community which comes under the TinkerHub Foundation. Currently there are many active campus communities all over Kerala and we are proud to be a part. Our aim is to enable a path for everyone to access the knowledge required to set the course for a better future. To start with, we help our students to learn and understand the importance of peer learning and the advantages of being part of a non-profitable organisation like TinkerHub.
